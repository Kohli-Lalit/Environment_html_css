Our Services

Greenhouse Gas Inventory

A GHG inventory is a comprehensive list of emission sources and associated emissions, quantified using standardized methods. It is an essential tool for managing GHG risks, identifying reduction opportunities, and participating in voluntary or mandatory GHG programs

Project Design for Carbon Credit

A PDD is a crucial component of developing carbon credit projects. It serves as a comprehensive blueprint explaining in detail how a project will go about reducing GHG emissions or sequester carbon. Creating a well-crafted PDD is essential for project validation and registration when applying for carbon credits with standard-setting bodies.

Net zero services

Our Net Zero advisory services help organizations achieve carbon neutrality by assessing current carbon footprints, developing customized strategies aligned with climate science, conducting cost-benefit analyses, and providing detailed implementation roadmaps. We also advise on high-quality carbon offset strategies for unavoidable emissions.

Water Footprint

The water footprint measures freshwater used in producing goods and services, including direct and indirect use. By assessing this footprint, we help clients improve water efficiency and reduce scarcity impacts, integrating these considerations into our carbon and net zero strategies for a holistic approach to sustainability.
